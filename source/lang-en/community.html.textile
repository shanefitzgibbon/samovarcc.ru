OUR CONTRIBUTION

We hope that our workshops people learn more tolerant attitudes in other cultures, and we also want to help those less fortunate than us. Help not only important it is also very interesting and fun!

See reports on how we have managed to do something. If you want to help with us, please write, call us - we will be very happy!

In December 2011, we took part in a charity bazaar Montessori school - we decorated cookies and kapkeyki, and all profit from ticket sales went to the Center for Curative Pedagogics. It was a beautiful, happy day, we want to repeat it this year.
